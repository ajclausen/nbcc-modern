## Sunday School at NBCC

Every Sunday at 9:00 AM, we offer Sunday School classes for every age group. This is a great opportunity for in-depth Bible study and discussion before our main worship service.

## Class Offerings

### Children's Classes
- **Nursery** (Birth - 2 years): Loving care with age-appropriate activities
- **Preschool** (3-5 years): Bible stories, songs, and crafts
- **Elementary** (K-5th grade): Interactive lessons and activities

### Student Classes
- **Middle School** (6th-8th grade): Relevant Bible study for tweens
- **High School** (9th-12th grade): Deeper discussions and faith exploration

### Adult Classes
We offer adult Sunday School with rotating topics and book studies. All adults are welcome regardless of where you are in your faith journey.

## Why Sunday School Matters

Sunday School provides:
- Focused time in God's Word
- Age-appropriate teaching
- Smaller group connections
- Deeper spiritual growth

Come at 9:00 AM and stay for our 10:00 AM worship service!
